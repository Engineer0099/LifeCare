About Us

Life Choice Services Ltd is a CQC-registered provider of domiciliary care services, delivering exceptional quality care with dignity and respect.

Our Story

Life Choice Services Ltd (trading as My Homecare) was established to provide high-quality, person-centred domiciliary care services tailored to individual needs.

Our DBS-checked carers support adults aged 18–65+ including those with dementia, disabilities, and terminal illnesses.

As a CQC-registered provider, we continuously improve our services to ensure safety, quality, and compassion.
