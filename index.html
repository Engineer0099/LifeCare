Who We Are

Life Choice Services Ltd (trading as My Homecare) is a CQC-registered provider of domiciliary care services. We're committed to delivering high-quality, person-centred care that helps individuals maintain their independence and quality of life.

Our team of trained, DBS-checked carers provide professional care with compassion and understanding. We support adults aged 18-65+ with various care requirements, including those with dementia, physical disabilities, and terminal illnesses.
